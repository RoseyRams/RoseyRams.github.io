Hi, I’m Cristina! I am a story artist and animator. I am working towards a BFA at the College for Creative Studies in Detroit, MI. I have worked on many small group animations, as well as completed my own animated film, “Mama”. I have also self-published a horror zine, town of weeds.
I love to animate using TV Paint, Adobe Animate, Dragonframe 4, and Toonboom Harmony.
I love to illustrate using Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Procreate.
I love to relax with a cup of warm chai and my cat Sophie.
